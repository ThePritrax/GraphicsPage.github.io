About Me

Hey, this is Priyansh Sinha, a Junior at Vellore Institute of Technology - AP. I am from Nagpur, Maharashtra but I was born in Patna, Bihar. I have done my schooling from Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan's Nagpur with good grades and equally good contribution in Extra Curricular activities.

Computer Science Student Pursuing B.Tech at VIT AP. Passionate about Programming, Development and Most Importantly Growing. A Creative Problem Solver, Avid Learner and Highly Capable Leader. Proficient in Java, C++, Data Structures, Algorithms. I have always had a knack for any creative field, be it as basic as drawing and sketching to a higher levels of Website Development, Designing and Graphic Designing. My mother got me into this, as she was an elementary school Creative Arts teacher, and I have ever since been fascinated by any opportunity to design or create.

My Work Experience

I have elementary proficiency in Web Development and Video Editing, high proficiency in Canva Designing and in Creative Arts such as Sketching, Drawing, Painting, Calligraphy etc.

I started exploring creative arts ever since I was a 7 year old kid, and have never stopped since. I started Graphics Designing on Canva in my 10th grade, when I got a brand new phone. I did the first few months of basic designing just for fun, but once my mom said "Try and find a way to monetize what you are good at." So as a person who used Instagram daily, I found a football graphics page with around 20,000 followers and started working for them, not for the money, but for my interest overlap of Football and new found Designs. Entering college, I have just learned Web Development in my 2nd year, and believe that I have a long way to go in this field. Regardless of my proficiency, whatever may the challenge be, if there is an opportunity to learn, I will tackle it head on an grab the opportunity with open arms.
